VINUR Kids Tumbler with Handle

A 14 oz insulated water cup made of food-grade 304 stainless steel, designed for kids who are always on the move.

Whether your child is playing at home, heading out for a trip, or staying busy in the car, the VINUR Kids Tumbler offers a reliable, safe, and durable way to stay hydrated. Its lightweight build makes it easy for little hands to carry, and the leak-proof lid with a built-in valve and silicone straw ensures that spills are a thing of the past. This tumbler is the perfect companion for kids who love to drink on the go.

Thanks to its advanced double wall vacuum insulation technology, this kids tumbler keeps drinks cold for up to 18 hours and hot for up to 8 hours. The thick, rounded handle is ergonomically designed to be comfortable and secure, while the soft rubber base helps prevent tipping and keeps the tumbler firmly in place. With a wide mouth for easy filling and cleaning, and a removable lid and straw for hassle-free maintenance, this is a must-have for any parent looking for a safe, leak-proof cup for their child.

Key Features

  • Food grade 304 stainless steel construction
  • Leak-proof lid with built-in valve and straw
  • Double wall vacuum insulation for cold and hot retention
  • Thick rounded handle for easy grip and carry
  • Removable lid and straw for hassle-free cleaning

Why Choose VINUR Kids Tumbler?

Safe, durable, and easy to clean, this travel drinkware is perfect for keeping your child hydrated throughout the day. The child-safe water cup is designed with both function and style, making it ideal for use at home, in the car, or during outdoor adventures.
